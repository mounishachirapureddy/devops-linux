## Process Management and Monitoring

- ps - report a snapshot of the current processes.
    > Ref: https://www.sysadmin.md/ps-cheatsheet.html
- top - display Linux processes.
    > Ref: https://gist.github.com/ericandrewlewis/4983670c508b2f6b181703df43438c37
- htop - interactive process viewer.
    > Ref: https://www.maketecheasier.com/power-user-guide-htop/
- atop - advanced interactive monitor to view the load on a Linux system.
    > Ref: https://neverendingsecurity.wordpress.com/2015/04/13/lsof-commands-cheatsheet/
- lsof - list open files.
